ABOUT US

Mission
We aspire to uplift badminton as a popular sport on campus, empowering players to excel through student-focused events, establishing us as a leading sports club at GITAM University.
Vision
We strive to be the preeminent voice at the nexus of badminton and empower the next generation of leaders to promote badminton as an influential tool among GITAM’s affluent youth.

The Badminton Club Vizag, a fully student-run organization overseen by the Directorate of Sports, develops beneficial student programs supported by its diverse mentor expertise and knowledge of badminton. With an outstanding crew we plan entertaining events and establish rules for stakeholders of the Badminton Club & GITAM Sports, leveraging the sports’ importance.
